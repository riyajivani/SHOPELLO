6 Easy DIYs To Help You Beat Your Skin Care Woes

Beautiful skin is the foundation of beauty. For a brighter and healthier skin, you need to follow some tips religiously to enhance your skin care regimen. Here is a list of some common problems you face and simple DIY remedies to rectify them.

1. Get Rid Of That Tan

Just grab some yogurt and apply it on the affected area. The trick is to let it stay for half an hour and then rinse it off with water. Rich in lactic acid, yogurt is an excellent anti-tanning agent. You can also use a mixture of egg and honey to clear skin tanning. Apply the mixture on the affected area, leave it on for twenty minutes and then wash it off with water.

2. Banish Dull Skin

We have enlisted easy and natural home remedies that should help you regain the missing glow from your skin. Mix rose water and glycerin with lemon juice and apply it daily for about 20 minutes before taking a shower. Rich in vitamin C and citric acid, lemons are helpful in brightening and lightening your skin. Another remedy you can opt for is a face mask made with honey and milk. Leave it on for 10 minutes and then wash it off.

3. Eliminate Dark Elbows and Knees

The dark elbows and knees annoy you? We feel you! All you need is some lime juice to even out the darkened elbows and bring back your natural skin tone. Cut some fresh lime wedges and simply rub them over your elbows and knees. Leave it for 20 minutes. Take a towel soaked in hot water and scrub the juice out post.

4. Dark Circles Be Gone

The skin around the eyes shows the first signs of aging and fatigue. Take a raw potato, wash it well and peel it off. Grate raw potato and place the shavings under your eyes for 10 minutes. Follow this for 7-8 days for the best effect.

5. Bye Brittle Nails

Just like our skin, our nails need moisturizing too on a regular basis. Soak your nails in olive oil for five minutes every day. This will moisturize your nails and also make them stronger.

6. Dry Lips No More

Mix Olive oil, sugar, & honey and spread this paste on your lips. This makes them super soft and let’s admit it, it doesn’t taste that bad either!
